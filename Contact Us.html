Millie's Tearoom in Hythe is within just a few miles of several top quality attractions and places of interest. Just 6 miles from Hythe is the National Motor Museum, home to over 250 vehicles, the World of Top Gear and with access to the Abbey and Palace House.

Equally close is the renowned Exbury Gardens and Steam Railway whilst just a little further afield are the beaches at Lepe which was prominent during D-Day preparations, and Calshot with its historic castle, both just minutes drive away.

Hythe itself, which dates back to Anglo Saxon times, is famous for its historic Pier upon which runs Britain's oldest continuously operating pier train.
