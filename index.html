Millie's Tearoom

Millie's Tearoom is a traditional style tearoom with an elegant feel. Located in the beautiful Waterside village of Hythe, on the shore of Southampton Water and the edge of the New Forest National Park, our pleasant, old-fashioned decor brings a unique charm to the area. We are a delightful, friendly team offering you a warm welcome and great customer service, making Millie's Tearoom the ideal place to meet friends and family.

We are often described as a hidden gem. Whether you want to try our locally sourced loose leaf teas and coffees or our homemade cakes, you won't be disappointed. Most popular are our afternoon teas, cream teas and homemade treats. We also offer gift vouchers and private hire on selected dates. Contact us for more information.
