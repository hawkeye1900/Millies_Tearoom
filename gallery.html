Our sandwiches are made fresh to order with a choice of farmhouse white, farmhouse wholemeal or gluten free bread. We have a selection of sandwich fillings including free range egg mayonnaise, mature cheddar and prawn Marie Rose.

We have a lovely range of teas to choose from including Twinings Fruit Teas, Earl Grey, Green tea and Peppermint Tea. We also stock a selection of loose leaf teas from our friends at The Guilded Teapot, an independent tea shop in Lymington. The lovely Dorest Brew and the Earl Grey Supreme being the most popular.

If you are not a tea drinker our delicious classic dark roast coffee will delight your taste buds. Locally freshly roasted in Southampton by our friends at Mozzo Coffee. Mozzo pride themselves in community and connection while minimising their environmental impact.

We serve delicious homemade cream teas and afternoon teas all day - who doesn't love a homemade scone with lashings of jam and clotted cream? Our afternoon tea served on beautiful tea stands, vintage plates and tea ware is a real treat. Each afternoon tea includes freshly made sandwiches accompanied by our cream tea and a generous slice of homemade cake.

Our light bites include homemade sausage rolls, quiches and our popular seasonal soups. Leek and potato, Roasted Tomato and Basil and delicious Mushroom soup are among the favourite soups.
